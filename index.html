why am i making this?

it's the first day of 2026 and i'm been depressed as hell!

i've spent so much time avoiding confronting my feelings and compartmentalizing for so long that i started feeling like nothing. hrt changed me and made me feel emotions for the first time in a while. in some ways i'm better off but other ways still a mess.

i amn't sure what i want from this website. i hope it can help me stop my bedrot and lack of motivation to do things. i want to write my ideas down in a more centralized spot, get better at expressing myself, keep track of what i create, and archive stuff about my father. i want something a bit more tangible and permanent than occasionally having things popping into my mind.

hopefully things work out.
